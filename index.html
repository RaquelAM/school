About Spanish to Go

Spanish to go is an agency that offers Spanish classes for foreigners. Our teachers are polyglot Language Studies graduates of UNAM (National Autonomous University of Mexico), with specializations in Linguistics and Spanish Language Teaching at CEPE (School for Foreign Students), in addition to a wide range of teaching experience.

We aim for clarity of the language by organizing and simplifying concepts in our explanations and course materials. We use creativity to make the learning process easier for the student, since this method is proven to generate stronger cognitive connections by working with our emotive nature and experiences. And finally, we are convinced that effectiveness is key to any language, that’s why we focus on the student’s context and needs, so that what is learned in class can be used in his daily life.
